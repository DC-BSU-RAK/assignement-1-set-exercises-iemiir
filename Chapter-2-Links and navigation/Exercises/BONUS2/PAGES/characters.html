Main Characters

Lucifer Morningstar

The Devil himself, who became bored with ruling Hell and decided to take a vacation in Los Angeles. He owns the nightclub Lux and becomes a civilian consultant for the LAPD. Lucifer has the power to draw out people's hidden desires and is charming, witty, and hedonistic. He struggles with understanding human emotions and his own developing feelings.

Lucifer's journey involves self-discovery, redemption, and understanding the concepts of free will and self-determination.

Chloe Decker

A dedicated LAPD homicide detective who is immune to Lucifer's powers. She's skeptical about Lucifer's claims of being the Devil but values his insight on cases. Chloe is logical, practical, and driven by a strong sense of justice.

Her character arc includes learning about her "miracle" birth and developing a deeper relationship with Lucifer despite his supernatural nature.

Mazikeen (Maze)

A fierce demon who followed Lucifer from Hell and initially serves as his loyal bodyguard and bartender at Lux. She is an expert torturer and fighter with proficiency in various weapons. Maze struggles with human emotions and finding her place on Earth.

Throughout the series, she develops friendships, learns about loyalty beyond servitude, and searches for her own purpose.

Amenadiel

God's firstborn son and Lucifer's older brother. He initially comes to Earth to bring Lucifer back to Hell but gradually becomes more connected to humanity. Amenadiel is strong, righteous, and initially judgmental but develops compassion and understanding.

His character experiences the loss and return of his angelic powers and explores themes of faith, worthiness, and fatherhood.

Dr. Linda Martin

Lucifer's therapist who helps him understand his emotions. Initially unaware that her patient is literally the Devil, she eventually learns the truth and continues helping him and other celestial beings. Linda is insightful, caring, and often serves as the voice of reason.

Her character balances professional ethics with supernatural friendships and deals with celestial parenthood.

Dan Espinoza

An LAPD detective and Chloe's ex-husband. He initially dislikes Lucifer but gradually develops a begrudging friendship with him. Dan struggles with corruption in his past and tries to be a good father to his daughter, Trixie.

His character deals with guilt, redemption, and the challenges of morality in complex situations.

Ella Lopez

The cheerful and religious forensic scientist who joins the LAPD in season 2. She's optimistic, hugs everyone, and maintains her faith despite challenges. Ella believes Lucifer is method acting when he claims to be the Devil.

Her character explores themes of faith, friendship, and finding light in darkness.

Trixie Espinoza

Chloe and Dan's young daughter who forms an immediate bond with Lucifer and Maze, despite their initial discomfort with children. She's intelligent, perceptive, and accepting of the unusual adults in her life.
