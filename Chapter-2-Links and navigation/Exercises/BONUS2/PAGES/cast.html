Main Cast

Tom Ellis as Lucifer Morningstar

Ellis portrays the charming Devil who leaves Hell to run a nightclub in Los Angeles. He later becomes a civilian consultant for the LAPD.

Lauren German as Detective Chloe Decker

German plays an LAPD homicide detective who partners with Lucifer on cases. She is initially skeptical of his claims to be the Devil.

Kevin Alejandro as Detective Dan Espinoza

Alejandro portrays Chloe's ex-husband and fellow detective who initially dislikes Lucifer but gradually develops a begrudging friendship with him.

D.B. Woodside as Amenadiel

Woodside plays Lucifer's older angel brother who initially comes to Earth to bring Lucifer back to Hell.

Lesley-Ann Brandt as Mazikeen (Maze)

Brandt portrays a fierce demon who followed Lucifer from Hell and works as his bodyguard and bartender at Lux.

Rachael Harris as Dr. Linda Martin

Harris plays Lucifer's therapist who helps him understand his emotions and eventually becomes aware of his true identity.

Aimee Garcia as Ella Lopez

Garcia portrays the cheerful and religious forensic scientist who joins the LAPD in season 2.

Creative Team

Tom Kapinos - Creator

Developed the series based on the DC Comics character.

Joe Henderson & Ildy Modrovich - Showrunners

Led the writing team and overall creative direction, particularly after the show moved to Netflix.

Jerry Bruckheimer - Executive Producer

The renowned producer whose production company helped bring the series to life.

Neil Gaiman - Original Character Creator

Created the character of Lucifer Morningstar in the comic book series "The Sandman."
