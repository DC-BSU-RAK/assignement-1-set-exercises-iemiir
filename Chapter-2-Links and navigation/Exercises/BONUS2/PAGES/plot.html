Plot

Bored as the Lord of Hell, Lucifer Morningstar abandons his throne for Los Angeles, where he runs the nightclub Lux and becomes a civilian consultant for the LAPD.

Lucifer uses his supernatural ability to draw out people's hidden desires while working with Detective Chloe Decker. He becomes intrigued by her immunity to his powers and her effect on his own vulnerability.

Throughout the series, Lucifer faces celestial family drama and supernatural threats while developing his relationship with Chloe and learning to understand human emotions.

Season 1

Lucifer helps Detective Chloe Decker solve crimes while dealing with his therapist Linda Martin and his brother Amenadiel, who wants to return him to Hell.

Season 2

Lucifer's mother escapes Hell and comes to Earth, causing complications. Lucifer struggles with his feelings for Chloe while uncovering more about her miraculous nature.

Season 3

Lucifer deals with the appearance of the world's first murderer, Cain (Marcus Pierce), while trying to reveal his true identity to Chloe.

Season 4

After Chloe learns the truth about Lucifer, their relationship changes. A prophecy about evil being released threatens everything.

Season 5

Lucifer's twin brother Michael arrives on Earth causing chaos, and God himself descends to mediate between his children.

Season 6

As Lucifer prepares to become God, he faces new challenges and must make difficult decisions about his future and relationships.
