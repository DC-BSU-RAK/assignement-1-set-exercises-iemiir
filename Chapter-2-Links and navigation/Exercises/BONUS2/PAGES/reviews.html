Critical Reception

The series received mixed reviews in its early seasons but gained stronger critical acclaim following its move to Netflix. Critics particularly praised the character development, the balance of humor with drama, and the exploration of existential and theological themes.

Fan Response

Lucifer developed a passionate fanbase that successfully campaigned for the show's revival after Fox's cancellation. The #SaveLucifer campaign demonstrated the show's dedicated following, which continued to grow during its Netflix run.

Legacy

The series is noted for successfully transitioning between networks and delivering a complete story with a planned ending. It has been praised for its unique take on celestial mythology and its exploration of redemption, free will, and self-determination.
