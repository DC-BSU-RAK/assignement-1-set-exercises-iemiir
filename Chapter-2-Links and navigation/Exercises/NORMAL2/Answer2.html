Overview

Lucifer is an American urban fantasy television series that ran for six seasons from 2016 to 2021, starting on Fox and concluding on Netflix.

Based on DC Comics character created by Neil Gaiman, the series follows Lucifer Morningstar, the Devil, who leaves Hell to run a nightclub in Los Angeles.

Plot

Bored as the Lord of Hell, Lucifer abandons his throne for Los Angeles, where he helps the LAPD solve crimes as a consultant.

Lucifer uses his ability to draw out people's desires while working with Detective Chloe Decker. He becomes intrigued by her immunity to his powers.

Throughout the series, Lucifer faces celestial family drama and supernatural threats while developing his relationship with Chloe.

Main Characters

Lucifer Morningstar - The Devil who becomes bored with Hell and vacations in Los Angeles. He can draw out people's hidden desires.

Chloe Decker - An LAPD detective immune to Lucifer's powers. She partners with Lucifer on cases.

Mazikeen (Maze) - A demon who followed Lucifer from Hell and serves as his bodyguard.

Amenadiel - Lucifer's older brother and an angel who initially tries to bring Lucifer back to Hell.
